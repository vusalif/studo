Advanced • 10 min read

Studo Pro unlocks advanced features designed for serious learners who want to maximize their study effectiveness. This guide covers all the premium features and how to use them effectively.

Enhanced Timer Features

Pomodoro Timer

Advanced Pomodoro technique with customizable work periods (15-60 minutes), short breaks (3-15 minutes), and long breaks after 4 sessions.

Session Analytics

Detailed breakdown of your study patterns, including peak productivity hours, session length trends, and focus quality metrics.

Pomodoro Configuration

  • Work Period: 15, 25, 30, 45, or 60 minutes
  • Short Break: 3, 5, 10, or 15 minutes
  • Long Break: 15, 20, 30, or 45 minutes
  • Auto-Start: Automatically begin breaks and work periods
  • Sound Alerts: Customizable notification sounds

Goal Setting & Progress Tracking

Set and track multiple types of goals: daily study time, weekly targets, subject-specific objectives, and long-term learning milestones.

Goal Types

  • Session Goals: Complete X study sessions
  • Subject Goals: Focus on specific topics or subjects
  • Streak Goals: Maintain daily study streaks
  • Custom Goals: Create your own objectives

Progress Visualization

  • Milestone Celebrations: Achievements and badges
  • Weekly Reports: Detailed progress summaries

Blurting Technique

The blurting technique is a powerful learning method where you immediately write down everything you remember about a topic after studying it. Studo Pro includes a dedicated blurting feature.

How to Use Blurting

  1. Study Session: Complete your normal study session
  2. Immediate Recall: Without looking at your notes, write everything you remember
  3. Compare & Review: Check your blurting against your study materials
  4. Identify Gaps: Note what you missed or got wrong
  5. Re-study: Focus on the areas you struggled with

Blurting Features

  • Timed Blurting: Set time limits for recall sessions
  • Topic Organization: Categorize blurting by subject
  • Progress Tracking: See improvement in recall accuracy
  • Export Options: Download your blurting notes

Active Recall Flashcards

Smart Spaced Repetition

Algorithm-based scheduling that shows cards at optimal intervals for long-term retention.

Multiple Card Types

Text, image, audio, and cloze deletion cards for different learning styles.

Creating Effective Flashcards

  • Question Format: Use clear, specific questions
  • Answer Clarity: Provide concise, accurate answers
  • Visual Elements: Add images and diagrams when helpful
  • Context: Include relevant context and examples

Spaced Repetition Algorithm

  • Easy Cards: Shown less frequently (4+ days)
  • Good Cards: Standard interval (1-3 days)
  • Hard Cards: Shown more frequently (same day)
  • Again Cards: Reset to beginning of sequence
