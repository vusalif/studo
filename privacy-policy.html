Privacy Policy

Privacy & Data Protection • Last updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Studo ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our study tracking application.

Information We Collect

Free Version

When using the free version of Studo:

  • Study Session Data: Timer durations, session notes, and study logs
  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally in your browser
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or behavior

Pro Version

When using the pro version of Studo:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and login
  • Study Data: All study sessions, notes, goals, and progress data
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Gumroad (we do not store payment details)
  • Cloud Backup: Your data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud

How We Use Your Information

Free Version

Your data never leaves your device. We have no access to your study information, notes, or any other data you create in the free version.

Pro Version

We use your information to:

  • Provide cloud backup and synchronization across devices
  • Generate analytics and progress reports
  • Send important account-related notifications
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Free Version Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your device
  • Browser Security: Data is protected by your browser's security measures
  • No Network Transmission: Your data never travels over the internet

Pro Version Security

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access data for support purposes
  • Regular Backups: Your data is regularly backed up to prevent loss

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Payment Processing: Gumroad processes payments (they have their own privacy policy)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Your Consent: Only with your explicit permission

Your Rights

Free Version

  • Complete Control: You can delete all data by clearing your browser storage
  • No Account Required: No personal information is collected
  • Local Management: You manage your own data locally

Pro Version

  • Access: You can view all your data through the application
  • Export: You can export your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information

Cookies and Tracking

Studo uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality
  • No Advertising: We do not use advertising cookies or tracking
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar services
  • Local Storage: Used to store your study data locally (free version)

Children's Privacy

Studo is designed to be safe for users of all ages:

  • No Age Verification: We do not collect age information
  • Educational Purpose: The app is designed for educational use
  • Parental Control: Parents can monitor usage through local data (free version)

Data Retention

Free Version

Data is retained as long as it exists in your browser's local storage. You can delete it at any time by clearing your browser data.

Pro Version

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days.

International Users

If you are using Studo from outside the United States:

  • Data Transfer: Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • GDPR Compliance: We comply with applicable data protection laws
  • Your Rights: You have the same rights regardless of your location

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time:

  • Notification: We will notify users of significant changes
  • Continued Use: Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Version History: Previous versions are available upon request

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: Contact us through the application or website
  • Data Requests: We respond to data requests within 30 days
  • Support: We provide support for all privacy-related inquiries
